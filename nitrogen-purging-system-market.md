<p><strong>The Nitrogen Purging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dilution Nitrogen Purging</li><li>Displacement Nitrogen Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nitrogen Purging System market comprises two main types: dilution nitrogen purging and displacement nitrogen purging. Dilution nitrogen purging involves the slow mixing of nitrogen with the hazardous gas to lower its concentration, enabling safer levels for processing. Conversely, displacement nitrogen purging pushes out harmful gases completely by replacing them with nitrogen, ensuring a contaminant-free environment. Both methods play critical roles in industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, enhancing safety and product integrity.</p></p>

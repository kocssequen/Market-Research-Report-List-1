<p><strong>The Integrated X-ray Sources Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Sealed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated X-ray sources are categorized into open type and sealed type. Open type sources feature an accessible radioactive material, allowing for customizable applications and flexibility in experimentation, but come with regulatory and safety considerations. Sealed type sources, on the other hand, enclose radioactive materials in protective casings, reducing contamination risks and improving safety. While open types offer versatility for research purposes, sealed types are preferred for their safety and ease of handling in industrial and medical applications.</p></p>

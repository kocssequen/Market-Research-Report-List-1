Parallax barrier technology employs a layered barrier that directs light to each eye, providing depth perception. In contrast, lenticular display technology uses an array of lenses to focus light, allowing multiple viewpoints and enhancing 3D effects. Both technologies cater to diverse applications, including gaming, advertising, and medical imaging, appealing to consumers seeking immersive visual experiences without traditional eyewear.</p></p>

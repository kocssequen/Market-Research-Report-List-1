<p><p>The environmentally friendly fume purifier market encompasses various technologies aimed at reducing airborne pollutants. Mechanical separation type uses physical barriers to filter particles, while catalytic combustion type utilizes catalysts to facilitate combustion and reduce harmful emissions. Activated carbon adsorption captures volatile organic compounds on carbon surfaces. Fabric filters trap particulates through woven materials, and wet processing type employs liquid mediums to scrub pollutants. Photocatalytic type uses light-activated catalysts to decompose contaminants, and electrostatic treatment type employs electric fields to attract and remove particles from the air.</p></p>

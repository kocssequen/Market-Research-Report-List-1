<p><p>The DC current rectifiers market can be categorized into three primary types: low-current, medium-current, and high-current rectifiers. Low-current rectifiers typically handle currents up to 10 amps, suitable for small electronic devices. Medium-current rectifiers, operating between 10 to 100 amps, are used in industrial applications where moderate power is required. High-current rectifiers, exceeding 100 amps, cater to heavy-duty applications like electric vehicles and industrial machinery, providing robust performance in demanding environments. Each type serves distinct applications based on current requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The Epoxy Active Diluent market is categorized into three main types: monofunctional, bifunctional, and others. Monofunctional types contain one reactive site, enhancing flexibility and reducing viscosity without significant crosslinking. Bifunctional types possess two reactive sites, promoting improved cure density and mechanical properties, making them suitable for structural applications. The "other" category encompasses various specialized diluents that cater to unique applications, offering tailored performance characteristics for diverse end-use markets, including coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>

<p><p>The antibacterial glass market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Soluble Antimicrobial Glass** releases antibacterial agents when in contact with moisture, providing ongoing protection.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Porous Antimicrobial Glass** features a structure that allows for enhanced antimicrobial activity by trapping bacteria within its pores. </p><p>3. **Coated Antibacterial Glass** is treated with a surface layer of antimicrobial substances, creating a protective shield against pathogens.</p><p>4. **Ion Diffusion Antibacterial Glass** incorporates antimicrobial ions that diffuse to the surface, actively inhibiting bacterial growth over time.</p></p>

<p><p>The Modified Bitumen Roofing System market comprises primarily two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene) modified bitumen. SBS systems offer enhanced elasticity and resistance to temperature fluctuations, making them suitable for various climates. APP systems, on the other hand, provide greater UV resistance and durability, ideal for areas with high sun exposure. Both types employ an asphalt-based membrane, ensuring long-lasting protection against water infiltrations, effectively catering to commercial and residential roofing needs.</p></p>

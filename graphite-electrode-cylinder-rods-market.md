<p><ul><li>Regular Power</li><li>High Power</li><li>Ultra High Power (UHP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite electrode cylinder rods are classified into three main types based on their power capacity: Regular Power, High Power, and Ultra High Power (UHP). Regular Power electrodes are suitable for standard electric arc furnaces. High Power electrodes offer improved current carrying capability, making them ideal for more demanding applications. Ultra High Power electrodes provide the highest conductivity and thermal resistance, designed for the most energy-intensive operations. Each type is tailored to meet specific performance needs in steel production and other metallurgical processes.</p></p>

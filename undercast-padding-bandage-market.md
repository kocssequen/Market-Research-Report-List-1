<p><p>The undercast padding bandage market comprises two main types: Flexible Fabric Bandages and Cohesive Fixation Bandages. Flexible Fabric Bandages are designed to cushion and protect underlying skin while accommodating movement, making them ideal for patients with casts or splints. Cohesive Fixation Bandages, on the other hand, adhere to themselves without sticking to skin or hair, ensuring secure placement and comfort. Both types serve critical roles in injury management, providing support and protection for various medical applications.</p></p>

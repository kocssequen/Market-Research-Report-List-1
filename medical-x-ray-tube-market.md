<p><strong>The Medical X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical X-Ray Tube market is primarily segmented into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed anode, making them simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for low-energy applications. In contrast, rotating anode tubes have an anode that spins to dissipate heat more effectively, allowing for higher energy and longer exposure times, making them ideal for advanced imaging techniques. Each type caters to different clinical needs and equipment requirements in medical diagnostics.</p></p>

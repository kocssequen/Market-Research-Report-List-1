<p><ul><li>Q-Switched Fiber Laser</li><li>Mode-Locked Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanosecond pulsed fiber laser market includes two primary types: Q-Switched Fiber Lasers and Mode-Locked Fiber Lasers. Q-Switched Fiber Lasers generate high-energy pulses by temporarily storing energy and releasing it in a short burst, ideal for applications like material processing and precise engraving. In contrast, Mode-Locked Fiber Lasers produce ultra-short, stable pulses with extremely high peak powers, suitable for applications in telecommunications, medical procedures, and scientific research. Both technologies drive innovation across various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The silicon aluminum alloy market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics and applications. Subcrystalline silicon aluminum alloys, characterized by fine silicon crystals, improve mechanical properties. Eutectic silicon aluminum alloys achieve a balanced phase composition, enhancing fluidity and reducing shrinkage in casting. Hypereutectic silicon aluminum alloys, with higher silicon content, provide improved wear resistance and lower thermal expansion. High silicon aluminum alloys, containing significant silicon, are often used in aerospace and automotive industries due to their strength and lightweight properties.</p></p>

<p><p>The ultrasonic scalpel market is categorized into three main types based on frequency: high frequency, low frequency, and other. High-frequency ultrasonic scalpels operate above 30 kHz, providing precise tissue cutting and coagulation with minimal thermal damage, making them ideal for delicate surgeries. Low-frequency scalpels, typically below 30 kHz, are used for more robust applications and thicker tissue, offering greater power. The "other" category encompasses niche devices and emerging technologies, catering to specialized surgical needs and innovations in ultrasonic cutting.</p></p>

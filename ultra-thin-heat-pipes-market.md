<p><p>The Ultra-Thin Heat Pipes market comprises primarily two types: round heat pipes and flat heat pipes. Round heat pipes are cylindrical in shape, commonly used in applications requiring high thermal conductivity in compact spaces, making them ideal for electronics cooling. Flat heat pipes, on the other hand, have a planar design, allowing for efficient heat spreading across surfaces. They are particularly suited for applications in consumer electronics and thermal management systems, where space constraints and heat dissipation are critical considerations.</p></p>

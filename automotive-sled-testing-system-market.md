<p><strong>The Automotive Sled Testing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Decelerator Sled Testing System</li><li>Catapult Sled Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Sled Testing System market encompasses two primary types: Decelerator Sled Testing Systems and Catapult Sled Testing Systems. Decelerator sleds simulate vehicle crashes by rapidly decelerating to test safety features and occupant protection, focusing on controlled impact analysis. In contrast, catapult sleds accelerate a vehicle to a predetermined speed before release, allowing for dynamic crash tests that replicate real-world collisions. Both systems are crucial for validating automotive designs and enhancing safety standards in vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>

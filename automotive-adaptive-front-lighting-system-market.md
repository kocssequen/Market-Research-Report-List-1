<p><p>The Automotive Adaptive Front Lighting System (AFS) market is primarily divided into two key types: High Beam and Dipped Headlight. High Beam systems adjust to provide maximum illumination for distant objects while minimizing glare for oncoming traffic. Dipped Headlights, or low beams, offer controlled lighting for close-range visibility without blinding other drivers. Both types enhance safety and driving comfort by adapting light distribution based on vehicle speed, steering angle, and road conditions, ensuring optimal visibility during nighttime driving.</p></p>
